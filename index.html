About

Analytical roots. Human-centred practice.

I'm a Product Designer at Quantium working on complex digital products. My background is unusual for a designer — I spent several years as a Senior Auditor at KPMG, which gave me a deeply analytical foundation I bring to every project.

I care about understanding the real problem before designing the solution, and I use AI tools strategically throughout my process — accelerating research synthesis, conceptual design and prototyping — to deliver higher quality work faster. AI supports the execution while I lead the strategy, design decisions and quality of the final output.
